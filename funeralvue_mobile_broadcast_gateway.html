FuneralVue :: Mobile Broadcast Gateway

This is a Proof-of-Concept (PoC) to demonstrate the broadcast of a live stream from Wowza GoCoder on an iOS or Android device.

Requirements

  • Pre-existing FuneralVue.com Login / Event Detail
  • Internet connection with upload speed of 1Mbps or greater
  • iOS: Wowza GoCoder on App Store
  • Android: Wowza GoCoder on Play Store

Broadcast Live Stream

For purposes of demonstration, this live stream is being used with event ID 26484. Click the publish link below that corresponds to your device type.

IMPORTANT NOTE: After GoCoder has launched, you may need to click the red button to begin streaming.
